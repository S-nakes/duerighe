About us

Due Righe was born from the vision of Sergio Ventricelli and Valentina Ventricelli, two professionals with years of experience in the sector. We combine creativity and technical expertise to transform each space into a unique experience. Our mission is to enhance each project with innovative and tailor-made solutions, guaranteeing quality and excellence.

Services

Marketing

We develop customized strategies to analyze consumer needs, identify ideal market segments and effectively position your brand. Through targeted campaigns and multi-channel communication, we amplify your presence on the market, increasing visibility and competitiveness.

Fittings

We create innovative and functional exhibition spaces for trade fairs, museums, events and showrooms. Our expertise spans from design to execution, ensuring that every detail reflects your brand identity and provides an immersive experience for visitors.

Internazionalization

We support companies in expanding into new global markets. We offer strategic advice for entering foreign markets, analyzing opportunities and adapting business strategies to address cultural and regulatory challenges, facilitating sustainable international growth.
